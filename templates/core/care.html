Expert Care Instructions

Maintain the beauty and value of your luxury products with our expert care tips. From watches to handbags, we guide you through proper maintenance.

Watches

  • Keep your watch clean and dry; avoid exposure to extreme temperatures.
  • Service your watch regularly with authorized professionals.
  • Store your watch in a protective case when not in use.

Handbags & Accessories

  • Store handbags in dust bags to prevent scratches and dust accumulation.
  • Avoid overloading to maintain shape and structure.
  • Clean with appropriate leather or fabric care products.

Apparel

  • Follow care labels for washing and dry cleaning instructions.
  • Store garments in breathable garment bags to prevent damage.
  • Handle delicate fabrics with care to avoid snags and tears.
